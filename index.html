About Us

Who we are

TK ALL IN ONE HOLDINGS AND PROJECTS is a proudly South African company owned by Katishi Ngoepe, dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions to all our clients. We are more than just a carpentry and steel fabrication company; we are your trusted partner in bringing your vision to life.

Our Story

Our journey began in 2018, initially focusing on steel products. However, we quickly recognized our clients' desire for a one-stop-shop solution. In response, we expanded our offerings to encompass a full range of design and manufacturing services, evolving into the TK ALL IN ONE HOLDINGS AND PROJECTS you see today.
